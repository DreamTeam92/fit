In autumn 2014 I took part in my first fitness competition and I started to work with Zoltan Vegh fitness photographers. One of the great success of my career was when one of my competitors in 2015 placed third in the World Championship in Dubai organized by the INBA (International Natural Bodybuilding Association).

In 2017 I`ve found myself in a weirdest condition ever. My body started to fall apart. I felt pain all over my body, not only when I worked out but in my day to day life too. Often during the night when I tried to turn to my other side the pain woke me up. That point I`ve realised something isn`t right in my body and I must fix it otherwise it might be worsen.

I`ve started researching, studying, attending courses, and worked together with Alex Szabo my Osteopath, to figure out what was wrong in with my body and how can we fix it.

Basically I had muscles imbalance all over my body, it didn`t functioned as it has been designed for. This lead to losing my correct posture and moving in a compensation pattern which caused me a pain. I had a pronation in my feet, excessive forward lean, bubbly knees during squat, anterior pelvic tilt, shifting pelvis, hunchback, forward shoulder, sciatica, and very tight muscles). Over these years I`m still on my mission to fix my body. Still not perfect but lot better than it was and I`m pain free.

It may sound weird but, I’m glad it happened, because I learned a lot about the human body, it`s function, and It helped me to be a better coach, and able to others.
