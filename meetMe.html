I have competed in fitness competitions, and worked with Zoltan Vegh Fitness photographers. One of the greatest successes in mycareer swas when when one of my competitors in 2015 placed third in the World Championship in Dubai organized by the INBA (International Natural Bodybuilding Association)

In 2017 after years of competitions and weight training, my body started to fall apart. I felt pain all over, not only when I worked out but in my everyday life as well. Often I felt pain during the night that woke me from sleep. I realised something was not right in my body and it had to be corrected before becoming a long lasting injury.

I started researching, studying, attending courses, and worked together with Alex Szabo, an Osteopath, to figure out what was wrong and how to correct it.

We determined I had muscle imbalance throughout my body, and I was losing correct posture. As a result key muscle groups were overcompensating for others, leading to the pain. Working with corrective exercises helped me overcome these issues and now I am pain free, but also having regular massage once-twice per week with a mobile massage therapist, helps heavily to maintain my muscle health.

With this experience, I learned a great deal about the human body, its proper function and a variety of different techniques to correct dysfunction. My coaching focusses on correcting stability, strength and overall performance in all parts of the body to help you achieve a happy and healthy lifestyle.
