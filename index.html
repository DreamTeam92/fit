About my
small business

Function Better Fitness and Pilates Training was created to design bespoke training for individuals and get the best result in their day to day life.

My aim was when I created my business to help busy individuals achieving their goals in their busy day to day life where everyone can fnd their training style which suit them best.

Muscle imbalance is one of the major problem in our population. Many of us has deskjob, traveling and communiting, also workout where lack of techniques and knowledge also can create musce disfunction without knowing it. Muscle dysfunction can lead over time injuries long term problems which some cases surgery unavoidable

This is where Corrective Exercise could help you finding imbalances, dysfunctions. By designing a bespoke training to individuals it could be minimise those risk factors significantly can improve life quality.
