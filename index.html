About my
small business

Function Better Fitness and Pilates Training was created to design bespoke training for individuals and get the best result in their day to day life.

As a Corrective Exercise Specialist, personal trainer, Pilates teacher with human foundations knowledge, I have solid a understanding of anatomy, kinesiology, and biomechanics to address and fix movement compensations, postural distortions and imbalances. My aim is to help people understand, their own body and improve the overall quality of movement during workouts and in everyday life.

With 17 years of experience in the fitness industry, I have found muscle imbalance as one of the major problems with most clients. Many of us have lifestyles with sedentary desk jobs, long commuting times and poor work out techniques, that create muscle dysfunction. Muscle dysfunction can lead to injuries and long term problems over time, often making surgery unavoidable.

I analyse your movement patterns, identify over or under active muscle groups and compensations. Once the assessment process is over, I create programs that optimise your ability to move, perform exercises correctly, and reduce tissue tension caused by the load put on the body via training.

You will be able to move better leading to a variety of new exercises and take part in activities and sports you enjoy, and may have not been able otherwise.

I create and teach quality workouts that enable clients to learn each exercise by correcting faulty movement patterns or wrong techniques.

My goal is to make you feel amazing.
