Body Shaping Class

This class specially designed to help you tone and sculpt muscles, strengthening your core and rounding your butt. Body Shaping class is combines a mix of resistance full body workout, core exercises, Pilates exercise and stretching.

 

Exercises involve completing reps using a choice of weights, resistance band, Pilates ball. This class vary from week to week to keep it fun and challenging. Regularly attending this class will help with conditioning, toning and sculpting your whole body to make you look and feel amazing.

 

Equipment may you need:

  • Light per of dumbbell (0,5-1kg)
  • Small or long light resistance band
  • Small Pilates ball
  • For all fitness level

Pilates Class

Promote strength, balanced, muscle development, flexibility and increase range of motion for the joints. Attention to the core support and full-body fitness, including the breathing and the mind. Provide a level of integrative fitness that is hard to find elsewhere.

 

My classes are includes many of the ideas I have trained with along the way, which enable you not only move safely and effectively within class, but also function correctly with stability in your day to day life, minimising risk of injury, and of course improving your overall strength, flexibility, muscle tone and posture.

 

My classes aim to move the body in all planes to allow better functional movement for your general daily activities through to your professional sports.

 

Equipment may you need:

  • Light per of dumbbell (0,5-1kg)
  • Small or long light resistance band
  • Small Pilates ball
  • For all fitness level

Fitness-HIIT Class

A great way to add some weight training, working the whole body, and burning a decent amount of calories- around 600 during 1 hour class. The class involves weighted reps to varying beats and speeds, working legs, back, arms, triceps and abs.

 

If you increase the weight, this class is great to push your strength, improve fitness and resistance ability, more than a simple body conditioning class. Fitnes-HIIT Involved functional training, metabolic and bodyweight exercises too.

 

You will boost your metabolism, and burn calories both during and after the class. You could build muscle by exercises using weights, it will tone and condition your muscles. It also Keeps you motivated.

 

Equipment may you need:

  • Medium-heavy per of dumbbell (3kg-6kg)
  • Medium-heavy kettlebell (4kg-12kg)
  • Light-medium resistance band
  • For all fitness level
